EVAL-CN0409-ARDZ
=================

Water Turbidity Measurement System.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0409` uses the :adi:`ADPD105` photometric front end with an 860 nm
infrared LED and silicon PIN photodiode network to measure water turbidity
levels from 0 to 1000 FTU (Formazin Turbidity Units). The system supports two
ISO 7027 turbidity measurement standards (ratio and non-ratio methods) plus the
GLI method.

With three-point calibration, typical accuracy is +/-0.50 FTU or +/-5% of
reading (whichever is greater), with a 0.05 FTU noise level. The photodiodes
are positioned at 90 degrees and 180 degrees to the LED for nephelometric and
transmission measurements respectively.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0409-ARDZ evaluation board
- :adi:`EVAL-ADICUP3029` controller board (or ADICUP360)
- PC with USB support
- LaMotte-style test vials
- Calibration solutions (e.g., HI88703-11, Oakton T100, or Cole Parmer kit)

Hardware Setup
--------------

- IR LEDs and photodiode detectors (DS1, DS2) positioned at 90 degrees and
  180 degrees.
- Mechanical vial holder for LaMotte-style test vials.
- Shield attaches to the :adi:`EVAL-ADICUP3029` controller board.

Software Setup
--------------

The ADICUP3029 + CN0409 Demo software is available for turbidity measurement
and calibration. Calibration points can be set using standard solutions at
values such as 0.10, 15.0, 100, 750, and 2000 NTU.
