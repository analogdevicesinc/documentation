EVAL-CN0240-SDPZ
=================

Bidirectional High Voltage Current Measurement.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0240` is a circuit for monitoring bidirectional current from sources
with DC voltages up to ±270 V with less than 1% linearity error. The system
measures current through an external shunt resistor with approximately 100 mV
maximum shunt voltage at full load.

The circuit uses the :adi:`AD629` difference amplifier for high common-mode
voltage rejection (up to 270 V), :adi:`AD8622` dual precision rail-to-rail
op-amp (gain of 100), :adi:`AD8475` fully-differential attenuating amplifier
(gain of 0.4), :adi:`AD7170` 12-bit sigma-delta ADC, :adi:`ADuM5402`
quad-channel digital isolator for galvanic isolation, and the :adi:`ADR435`
precision XFET voltage reference.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0240-SDPZ evaluation board
- :adi:`EVAL-SDP-CB1Z` controller board (SDP-B board)
- +6 V power supply (1 A)
- Dual power supply: +15 V and -15 V
- USB cable
- External shunt resistor (100 mV max at full load)
- Source voltage and electronic load

.. warning::

   This is a high voltage circuit (up to ±270 V). Only qualified professionals
   should operate this equipment.

Hardware Setup
--------------

- **J1** -- 120-pin SDP connector.
- **J2** -- +6 V barrel connector (wall wart input).
- **J3** -- +6 V DC power supply input.
- **J4** -- High voltage input terminals (±270 V max) for shunt resistor.
- **J5** -- ±15 V supply input.
- System ground and PCB isolated ground must be connected (test points 31
  and 32) for correct operation.

Software Setup
--------------

The evaluation software provides data acquisition, error calculation, and
graphing functions for bidirectional current measurement.
