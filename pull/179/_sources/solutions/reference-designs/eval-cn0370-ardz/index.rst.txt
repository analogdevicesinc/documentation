EVAL-CN0370-PMDZ
=================

Low Noise LED Current Source Driver.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0370` is a single-supply, low noise LED current source driver controlled
by a 16-bit DAC. The circuit maintains ±1 LSB integral and differential
nonlinearity with 0.1 Hz to 10 Hz noise of less than 45 nA peak-to-peak for
20 mA full-scale output current.

The circuit uses the :adi:`AD5542A` 16-bit serial-input voltage-output DAC, the
:adi:`ADA4500-2` single-supply dual low-power op-amp with zero-crossover
distortion and rail-to-rail input, and the :adi:`ADR4525` 2.5 V high-precision
low-noise voltage reference.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0370-PMDZ evaluation board
- :adi:`EVAL-SDP-CB1Z` controller board (SDP-B board)
- SDP-PMD-IB1Z (SDP-to-PMOD interposer board)
- 6 V wall wart power supply
- USB cable
- Agilent 34401A multimeter or equivalent

Hardware Setup
--------------

- Connect 120-pin connector J4 on the interposer to CON A on the SDP-B board
  with JP1 set to +5.0 V.
- Connect the EVAL-CN0370-PMDZ to PMOD connector J3 on the interposer.
- Connect +6 V wall wart to connector J1 of the interposer board.
- LED_SUPPLY can use PMOD supply (default) or external VEXT (0 to +20 V max).
- Test points VCC and AGND for voltage measurements.

Software Setup
--------------

The evaluation software provides two input modes: Current Mode for adjusting
output current via slider or text box, and Code Mode for writing exact DAC
codes. The external resistor parameter R_LIMIT (default 124 Ohm) can be
configured in the GUI.
