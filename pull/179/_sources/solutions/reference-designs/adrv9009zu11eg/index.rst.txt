ADRV9009-ZU11EG User Guide
===========================

Introduction
------------

The ADRV9009-ZU11EG HDL reference design is built around the Zynq UltraScale+
MPSoC. The design integrates two :adi:`ADRV9009` wideband RF transceivers with
a total of 8 transmit, 4 receive, and 4 observation/sniffer data paths via
JESD204B interface.

The PS provides SDIO, UART, Ethernet, SPI, USB 3.0, QSPI, and DisplayPort
control modules. Two PL DDR4 (32-bit @ 1200 MHz) modules can be used as
offload FIFOs.

Additional transceiver lanes are available for PCIe Gen3 x8, 10 Gb Ethernet,
and 40 Gb Ethernet simultaneously. A full FMC HPC connector enables extending
the system with another two ADRV9009 devices.

Supported Devices
-----------------

- :adi:`ADRV9009`

Supported Carriers
------------------

- ADRV2CRR-FMC Carrier Board

HDL Reference Design
--------------------

The digital interface consists of 8 transmit, 4 receive, and 4
observation/sniffer lanes running up to 9.8 Gbps. The DAC data may be sourced
from an internal data generator (DDS or pattern) or from the external DDR via
DMA. The ADC data is sent to the DDR via DMA.
