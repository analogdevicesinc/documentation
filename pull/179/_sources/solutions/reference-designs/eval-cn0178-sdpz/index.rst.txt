EVAL-CN0178-SDPZ
=================

Wideband RF Power Detector.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0178` measures RMS signal strength of RF signals with varying crest
factors over approximately 65 dB dynamic range, operating from 50 MHz to 9 GHz.

The circuit uses the :adi:`ADL5902` TruPwr RF power detector (50 MHz to 9 GHz,
-62 dBm to +3 dBm input range) and the :adi:`AD7466` 12-bit high-speed
successive approximation ADC (200 kSPS).

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0178-SDPZ evaluation board
- :adi:`EVAL-SDP-CB1Z` controller board (SDP-B board)
- +6 V power supply or wall wart
- USB cable
- RF signal source
- Coaxial RF cable with SMA connectors

Hardware Setup
--------------

- Connect 120-pin connector on EVAL-CN0178-SDPZ to CON A on SDP-B board.
- **J1** -- +6 V terminal block.
- **J2** -- +6 V barrel connector for wall wart.
- **J3** -- SMA connector for RF input.

Software Setup
--------------

The evaluation software provides 4-point calibration using known signal levels,
data acquisition with sample/remove/clear/save controls, and analysis with
XY plot of ADC code vs. output error.
