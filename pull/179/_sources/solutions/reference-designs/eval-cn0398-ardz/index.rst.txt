EVAL-CN0398-ARDZ
=================

Soil Moisture, pH, and Temperature Measurement.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0398` is a single-supply, low-power, high-precision solution for soil
moisture (capacitive), pH, and temperature measurements with temperature
compensation. The system uses capacitive soil moisture sensors that are
insensitive to water salinity and do not corrode over time.

The circuit uses the :adi:`AD7124-8` 24-bit sigma-delta ADC, :adi:`ADR3433`
precision voltage reference, :adi:`ADP7118` CMOS low-dropout linear regulator,
and the :adi:`ADA4661-2` dual precision rail-to-rail op-amp.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0398-ARDZ evaluation board (Arduino shield)
- :adi:`EVAL-ADICUP360` development board
- 7--12 V / 1 A DC power supply
- 3-wire capacitive moisture sensor (e.g., Vegetronix VH400 or Decagon EC-5)
- pH sensor with BNC connector (e.g., Atlas Scientific pH probe)
- Pt100 RTD for temperature
- USB cable
- PC with serial terminal software

Hardware Setup
--------------

- **J1** -- BNC connector for high-impedance pH probe.
- **P2** -- 3-wire moisture sensor connector (selectable 3.3 V or 5 V supply
  via jumpers P8/P10).
- **P1** -- RTD connector supporting 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire configurations
  (Pt100/Pt1000, 500 uA excitation current).
- **P5** -- Chip select configurable to GPIO pins 8, 9, or 10 (default
  GPIO 10).
- **P8** -- Output voltage selection (3.3 V or 5 V).
- **P10** -- Power supply selection (5 V or 7--12 V input).

Software Setup
--------------

The demo firmware provides three independent measurement channels with
precision biasing for the pH probe (internal bias voltage generator), soil
moisture measurement (0--3 V range), and temperature compensation via RTD.
