EVAL-CN0289-SDPZ
=================

Loop-Powered 4--20 mA Pressure Sensor Transmitter.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0289` is a robust 2-wire loop-powered current transmitter that converts
the differential voltage output from a pressure sensor to a 4--20 mA current
output. The circuit is optimized for bridge-based voltage or current-driven
pressure sensors with only four active devices, achieving less than 1% total
unadjusted error. Loop supply voltage range is 12 V to 36 V.

The input is ESD-protected and rated for over-voltage conditions.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0289-EB1Z evaluation board
- Power supply: +7 V to +24 V (minimum 7 V for headroom)
- Agilent 3458A or equivalent current meter
- Differential pressure sensor (e.g., NXP pressure sensors) or dual-channel
  DC signal source for simulation

Hardware Setup
--------------

- **J1** -- 4-channel screw terminal for bridge-type sensor connection
  (Vsense and V/Iforce pins)
- **J2** -- Power supply connection (+7 V to +24 V)
- **S1** -- Switch for selecting voltage vs. current drive mode

  - Voltage drive: VDRIVE = 10 V, supply must be >10.2 V
  - Current drive: supply must be >11.2 V minimum

- **R7** -- 250 Ohm series load resistor (desolderable for external load)

.. note::

   No evaluation software is required. This is a hardware evaluation board
   with direct measurements via a current meter or multimeter.
