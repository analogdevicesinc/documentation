EVAL-CN0251-SDPZ
=================

Flexible Wide Dynamic Range Signal Conditioner.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0251` is a flexible signal conditioning circuit for processing signals
of wide dynamic range (several mV to 20 V peak-to-peak), with programmable gain
amplifier and level shifting.

The circuit uses the :adi:`AD7192` precision sigma-delta ADC with programmable
gain and the :adi:`AD8475` fully-differential attenuating amplifier for signal
conditioning.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0251-SDPZ evaluation board
- :adi:`EVAL-SDP-CB1Z` controller board (SDP-B board)
- +6 V power supply
- ±15 V dual power supply
- USB cable
- :adi:`ADALM2000` measuring instrument (optional, for signal generation)

Hardware Setup
--------------

- **J5** -- 120-pin connector to SDP-B board.
- **J3** -- +6 V power supply screw terminals.
- **J4** -- ±15 V power supply screw terminals.
- **JP1, JP2** -- Configure analog inputs (single-ended or differential).
- **JP3, JP4** -- Configure AD8475 gain (0.4x or 0.8x).

Software Setup
--------------

The evaluation software provides channel selection, input range configuration,
display units conversion, and data export to tab-delimited ASCII files. Signal
generation can be driven by the ADALM2000 with Scopy software.
