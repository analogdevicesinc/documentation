EVAL-CN0188-SDPZ
=================

Current Monitoring for -48 V Systems.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0188` monitors current in individual channels of -48 V systems to
better than 1% accuracy, operating on a single +3.3 V supply.

The circuit uses the :adi:`AD7171` very low power 16-bit sigma-delta ADC,
the :adi:`ADuM5402` quad-channel digital isolator for optional galvanic
isolation, the :adi:`ADA4051-2` CMOS micropower zero-drift op-amp, and the
:adi:`ADR381` precision 2.5 V band gap voltage reference.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0188-SDPZ evaluation board
- :adi:`EVAL-SDP-CB1Z` controller board (SDP-B board)
- +6 V DC power supply or wall wart
- USB cable
- Shunt resistor (50 mV max at maximum load current)
- Electronic load

Hardware Setup
--------------

- Connect the CN0188 board to the SDP-B board via the 120-pin CON A connector.
- Connect shunt resistor across the input terminals with load to ground.
- Apply +6 V power via the +6 V and GND pins (or barrel connector for wall
  wart).
- System ground and PCB isolated ground must be connected (test points 31
  and 32) for correct operation.

Software Setup
--------------

The evaluation software provides shunt voltage input (in millivolts),
temperature setting for test conditions, data acquisition with sample/remove/
save controls, and measurement output display.
