EVAL-CN0295-SDPZ
=================

Flexible 4--20 mA Pressure Sensor Transmitter.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0295` is a flexible 4 mA to 20 mA pressure sensor transmitter that
converts the differential voltage output from a pressure sensor to a 4--20 mA
current output. The circuit is optimized for bridge-based voltage or current
driven pressure sensors with total unadjusted error less than 1%. Loop supply
voltage range is 7 V to 36 V.

The input is ESD-protected and rated for over-voltage conditions. The circuit
supports both voltage drive and current drive modes selectable via switch S1.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0295-EB1Z evaluation board
- Power supply: +7 V to +24 V
- Agilent 3458A or equivalent current meter
- Differential pressure sensor or equivalent dual-channel DC signal source

Hardware Setup
--------------

- **J3** -- 4-channel screw terminal for bridge-type sensor connection (Vsense
  and V/Iforce pins).
- **J2** -- Power supply connection (+7 V to +24 V, minimum 7 V for headroom).
- **S1** -- Switch for selecting voltage vs. current drive mode.

  - Voltage drive: VDRIVE = 10 V, supply must be >10.2 V.
  - Current drive: supply must be >11.2 V minimum.

- **R14** -- 500 Ohm onboard load resistor (desolderable for custom load).
- **P1** -- Terminal block for output voltage measurement across 250 Ohm series
  resistor.
- **J1** -- Male header for shunt current measurement.

.. note::

   No evaluation software is required. This is a hardware evaluation board
   with direct measurements via a current meter or multimeter.
