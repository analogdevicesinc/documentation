EVAL-CN0187-SDPZ
=================

RF Power Measurement with Crest Factor.

Overview
--------

:adi:`CN0187` is a crest factor, peak, and RMS RF power measurement circuit
optimized for high speed, low power, and single 3.3 V supply operation. The
system measures peak and RMS power at RF frequencies from 450 MHz to 6 GHz
over approximately 45 dB dynamic range.

The circuit uses the :adi:`AD629` difference amplifier, :adi:`AD8622` dual
precision rail-to-rail op-amp, :adi:`AD8475` fully-differential attenuating
amplifier, :adi:`AD7170` 12-bit sigma-delta ADC, :adi:`ADuM5402` quad-channel
digital isolator, and the :adi:`ADR435` precision voltage reference.

Required Equipment
------------------

- EVAL-CN0187-SDPZ evaluation board
- :adi:`EVAL-SDP-CB1Z` controller board (SDP-B board)
- +6 V power supply or wall wart
- USB cable
- RF signal source (>6 GHz capable)
- Coaxial RF cable with SMA connectors

Hardware Setup
--------------

- Connect 120-pin connector from EVAL-CN0187-SDPZ to CON A on SDP-B board.
- Connect RF signal source via SMA RF input connector.
- Apply +6 V power to the +6 V and GND pins (or use barrel jack with wall
  wart).

Software Setup
--------------

The evaluation software provides calibration, power measurement, and graphical
output in dBm, Vrms, and crest factor units.
