Hardware setup

On most carriers, the EVAL-AD488X-FMC board connects to the FMC LPC connector (unless otherwise noted). The carrier setup requires power, UART (115200), Ethernet (Linux), HDMI (if available) and/or JTAG (no-OS) connections. A few typical setups are shown below.
