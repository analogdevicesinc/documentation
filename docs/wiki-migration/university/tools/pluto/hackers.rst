Hacking the ADALM-PLUTO
=======================

`Hacking <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hacker_culture>`__ the ADALM-PLUTO is possible (and encouraged). In this case - what we mean by *hacking* is the act of programming or extending the functionality or use cases of the PlutoSDR, in a spirit of playfulness and exploration. In order to do that, you should be familiar with the device, from a `user <https://wiki.analog.com/users>`__ and `developer <https://wiki.analog.com/developers>`__ standpoint.

Many aspects of the PlutoSDR are based from the :doc:`AD-FMCOMMS3-EBZ </wiki-migration/resources/eval/user-guides/ad-fmcomms3-ebz>`, and you should be familiar with that, as it will help understand how to extend the PlutoSDR.
