Introduction
============

The :adi:`AD-FMCDAQ1-EBZ <en/design-center/evaluation-hardware-and-software/evaluation-boards-kits/eval-ad-fmcdaq1-ebz.html#eb-overview>` module is comprised of the :adi:`AD9684 <en/products/analog-to-digital-converters/high-speed-ad-10msps/ad9684.html#product-overview>` dual, 14-bit, 0.5 GSPS, LVDS ADC, the :adi:`AD9122 <en/products/digital-to-analog-converters/da-converters/ad9122.html#product-overview>` dual, 16-bit, 1.2 GSPS, LVDS DAC, the :adi:`AD9523-1 <en/products/clock-and-timing/clock-generation-distribution/ad9523-1.html#product-overview>` clock, and power management components. It is clocked by an internally generated carrier platform via the FMC connector, comprising a completely self contained data acquisition and signal synthesis prototyping platform. In an FMC footprint (84 mm × 69 mm), the module’s combination of wideband data conversion, clocking, and power closely approximates real-world hardware and software for system prototyping and design, with no compromise in signal chain performance. For additional information or to purchase the AD-FMCDAQ1-EBZ, please visit: www.analog.com/AD-FMCDAQ1-EBZ

Features
========

-  Includes schematics, layout, BOM, Gerber files, HDL, Linux® drivers, IIO Oscilloscope, VisualANALOG,...
-  FMC-compatible form factor
-  Powered from single FMC connector
-  Provides two channels of ADC and two channels of DAC with full synchronization capabilities

AD9684 ADC
==========

-  14-bit 500MSPs LVDS
-  SFDR: 85 dBc at 170 MHz AIN, 0.5 GSPS
-  Noise density: –153 dBFS/Hz @ 0.5 GSPS
-  2 GHz of usable analog input full power bandwidth
-  Two integrated DDCs per channel

AD9122 DAC
==========

-  16-Bit 1200MSPs LVDS
-  Supports complex signal bandwidths up to 800 MHz --- EDIT
-  6-carrier GSM IMD = 75 dBc at 75 MHz IF --- EDIT
-  SFDR = 85 dBc (BW = 300 MHz) at DC IF --- EDIT
-  Selectable 2×, 4×, 8× interpolation filters
