Arduino Compatible connector P2,3,4,5
=====================================

This connector provides the ADAS1021 digital interface pins to connect to a host microcontroller. It also provides power to the evaluation board through the 5V and 3V3 pins on port P2 which will be supplied by all microcontroller that comply with the Arduino R3 standard.

+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| Header | Pin No | Mnemonic        | Device  | Description                          |
+========+========+=================+=========+======================================+
| P2     | 1      | ECG_RESETB      | both    | Reset                                |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P2     | 2      | SLV_GPIO1       | slave   | Multipurpose pin                     |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P2     | 3      | SLV_GPIO2       | slave   | Multipurpose pin                     |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P2     | 4      | PMU_ENPG        | master  | Power                                |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P2     | 5      | SLV_GPIO3       | slave   | Multipurpose pin                     |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P2,P3  | 6      | SLAVE_SPI_CSB   | slave   | Active Low Chip Select Or Frame Sync |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P3     | 2,4    | 3V3_SHIELD      | master  | 3V Power                             |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P3     | 3      | TP16            | slave   | Test point                           |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P3     | 5      | 5V0_SHIELD      | master  | 5V Power                             |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P3     | 6,7    | DGND            | both    | Digital ground                       |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P3     | 2,4    | 3V3 Shield      | both    | VIN power supply to ADP5320          |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P3     | 5      | 5V0 Shield      | -       | VBATI ADP5320                        |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P3     | 6,7    | DGND            | -       | Digital Ground                       |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 1      | I2C_SCL         | ADP5320 | PMU I2C clock                        |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 2      | I2C_SDA         | ADP5320 | PMU I2C data                         |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 4      | DGND            | both    | Digital Ground                       |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 5      | SPI CLK         | both    | SPI clock                            |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 6      | SPI_SDO         | both    | SPI digital output                   |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 7      | SPI_SDI         | both    | SPI digital input                    |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 8      | Master_SPI_CSB  | both    | Master chip select                   |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 9      | GPIO3           | master  | GPIO3 Master                         |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P4     | 10     | GPIO5           | master  | GPIO5 Master                         |

| P5     | 1      | GPIO4           | master  | GPIO4 Master                         |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P5     | 2      | GPIO0/Timestamp | master  | Timestamp input                      |

| P5     | 3      | PMU_INT         | ADP5320 | PMU interrupt pin                    |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P5     | 4      | STOP2           | master  | PMU GPIO2                            |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
| P5     | 5      | GPIO1/IRQ       | master  | GPIO or interrupt output             |
+--------+--------+-----------------+---------+--------------------------------------+
