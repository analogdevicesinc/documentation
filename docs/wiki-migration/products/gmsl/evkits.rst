Blinking an LED
---------------

The simplest (and most satisfying) first step on most embedded platforms is to blink an LED to help confirm functionality. After you board is operating and connected to the GMSL GUI, we can begin with :doc:`Blinking an LED </wiki-migration/products/gmsl/evkits/blinking-an-led>`.
